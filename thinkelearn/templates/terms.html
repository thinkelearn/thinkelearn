Course Enrollment and Payment

Pricing Types

Courses on our Site may be offered under the following pricing models:

  • Free: No payment required. Enrollment is activated immediately upon registration.
  • Fixed-Price: A set price in Canadian Dollars (CAD) is required for enrollment.
  • Pay-What-You-Can: You choose your payment amount within a minimum and maximum range displayed on the course page.

Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through Stripe, a third-party payment processor. We do not store your credit card information on our servers. By making a payment, you agree to Stripe's terms of service and privacy policy.

Enrollment Activation

For paid courses:

  • Your enrollment will be in "Pending Payment" status until payment is successfully processed.
  • Upon successful payment, your enrollment status will change to "Active" and you will receive immediate access to course content.
  • If payment fails, your enrollment will be marked as "Payment Failed" and you may attempt to complete payment again.

Free courses and pay-what-you-can courses with a $0 amount will activate immediately without payment processing.

Enrollment for Children Under 13

Special requirements apply for enrollments involving children under 13 years of age, in compliance with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA):

Authentication Requirement:

  • Family Account Required: Children under 13 must access our platform using a COPPA-compliant family account from Google Family Link or Microsoft Family Safety. Direct signup with email and password is not available for children under 13.
  • Parental Consent Through Identity Provider: By using Google Family Link or Microsoft Family Safety, parents have already provided verifiable consent to the identity provider for their child to access educational services. We rely on this consent mechanism.
  • Age Verification: During signup, users must provide their date of birth. If the user is under 13, they will be directed to sign in using a family account instead of creating a direct account.

Account Restrictions:

  • No Direct Account Creation: Children under 13 cannot create accounts using email and password on our platform
  • OAuth-Only Access: Children under 13 can only access our platform by signing in through Google Family Link or Microsoft Family Safety accounts
  • Limited Features: Accounts authenticated through family accounts for children under 13 do not have access to certain features including direct payment processing or SMS notifications

Payment for Children's Enrollments:

  • Parent-Managed Payments: Payment for courses accessed by children under 13 must be processed by parents through their own accounts or through school/organizational billing
  • No Direct Child Payments: Children under 13 cannot process payments directly on our platform
  • Family Subscriptions: Parents may handle course fees through family account subscriptions or direct parent accounts

Parental Controls:

  • Access Management: Parents can revoke their child's access to THINK eLearn at any time through their Google Family Link or Microsoft Family Safety dashboard
  • Data Requests: Parents can request to review, export, or delete their child's educational data by contacting our Privacy Officer
  • Activity Monitoring: Parents can monitor their child's course activity through family account tools and by contacting us for educational progress reports

School and Organizational Access:

Schools using Google Workspace for Education or Microsoft 365 Education can provide access for students under 13 through their managed accounts, which include appropriate parental consent mechanisms. Schools should contact us at info@thinkelearn.com to arrange organizational access.

For complete details on how we handle children's personal information, please see the "Children's Privacy" section of our Privacy Policy.

Cancellation and Refunds

Refund Policy

We offer a refund window for paid course enrollments to ensure your satisfaction. The standard refund window is 30 days from the date of purchase, though some courses may have different refund windows as specified on the course page at the time of enrollment.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund within the applicable refund window:

  1. Log in to your student dashboard at /dashboard/
  2. Navigate to the course you wish to refund
  3. Click the "Request Refund" button (available only within the refund window)
  4. Optionally provide feedback about your reason for the refund

Refunds are processed automatically and typically appear in your original payment method within 5-10 business days, depending on your financial institution.

Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued at our discretion in special circumstances. Please contact us at support@thinkelearn.com to discuss your situation.
