Parent Help

Family account guidance for Google Family Link and Microsoft Family Safety.

OAuth-only access

THINK eLearn uses Google and Microsoft accounts for all sign-ins. We do not collect passwords, dates of birth, or age information.

If your child is under 13, please use a parent-managed family account so you can control access and consent through your provider.

Google Family Link

Family Link lets parents create and manage Google accounts for children under 13.

  • Create a family group and child account in the Family Link app.
  • Review and manage app permissions from the parent dashboard.
  • Use the child account to sign in to THINK eLearn with Google.

Learn more at families.google.com/familylink.

Microsoft Family Safety

Family Safety lets parents manage Microsoft accounts for children and family groups.

  • Create a family group and add your child account in Microsoft Family Safety.
  • Control screen time, app access, and privacy settings in the parent dashboard.
  • Use the child account to sign in to THINK eLearn with Microsoft.

Learn more at account.microsoft.com/family.

Managing access

Parents can control and revoke access at any time through the provider dashboard.

  • Review the child account activity through Family Link or Family Safety.
  • Revoke THINK eLearn access from your provider settings.
  • Contact us for data review, export, or deletion requests.

Contact our Privacy Officer

We respond to verified parent requests within 30 days.
